Target Audience

Personas

1) Julianne the investor
Julianne is looking for businesses in the area for her company to invest in. She wants to know what businesses are in the area, and what businesses attract the most attention.

2) Mark the small business owner
Mark is looking to advertise his small business in his local area and maybe expand his business to involve some larger businesses to expand attraction and revenue to his business.

Scenarios

1) I want to start a business, where do I start?

2) What will the weather be like next week? I am planning a family reunion.

3) I want to cut costs for my business, who can I reach out to?

4) Are there any activities that I can take my kids to that are fun, but educational?

5) What businesses are popular right now in the area?
