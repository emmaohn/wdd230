Discovery Page

With the boost in tourism to the area, our taxi fleet has about doubled in the past two years!

Skyline tours are available at the Pleasant Point Plaza in Porter Square.

The weather in Pleasant Point is typically misty and a little drizzly with a rain level of 15 feet a year, so bring an umbrella!

With our majority Chinese immigrant population, our Chinese sector is a great place to come enjoy the culture of our little town!

The night life in Pleasant Point is always thriving, with many good places to eat opening late and staying open even later, downtown Pleasant is the place to be for the night life scene!

Tourism has greatly affected the amount of public transportation that has begun to pop up in downtown pleasant, as well as the amount of hotels that are available for tourists!

The ferry ride to Isle Pleasant is one of our greatest attractions! The Isle features a museum displaying Pleasant Point's history, and the wives tales of our town.

Our tourism ratings have increased reapidly these last couple years, being rated #2 by several news sources! Our hospitality extends beyond our pleasant attitudes! Check out our five star rated hotels, restaurants and tourist attractions!

Helicopter tours are available at the Porter Plaza. Tours last for about 30 minutes to an hour, and depart and return to the landing pad on top of the Porter Plaza Bank
