Repetition

adidas.com

There is repitition in the button styling, the fonts, font sizing and all of the sections have similar themes throughout. Black and white are repeated throughout the site as well which give good color continuity.

Hick's Law

epicgames.com

There is one large button on the home page, no matter what size it is. If someone is unfamiliar with the content on hte page, they will feel confident in clicking a big button that say "Learn more." It is a simple page that indicates a simple next step.

Rule of Thirds

office.com

The focus is placed on the tablet in the image because it is placed in the right two thirds of the image, rather than directly in the center. This draws attention to the product.
