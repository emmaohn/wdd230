Commitment To Nature

Every year, nearly 13 million tons of textile waste is discarded into landfills, and this is only partially due to commercial retailers. The average citizen of the Earth plays a large role in the generation of textile waste, and there is something we can do about it, and if you're here, you'll like the solution! No doubt that ever since you were small, you knew what the thrift store was, or you dreaded that the only new clothes you would get that year would be from your older siblings. Well the era of being embarrassed by secondhand clothing is coming to a close. Not only is shopping for or wearing secondhand clothing minimizing the impact we have on the Earth, but it is also a cheap ticket to feeling confident in your second skin: what you wear. The way we dress is much more important than we may realize (you can read more about this phenomenon here). By even just encouraging others to think more about secondhand options, you can be the beginning of a new era for our Earth and for yourself. Feeling confident in your own skin (your first, and second ;)) is a priority that we often push aside because of the events and situations outside of us telling us that our self-esteem can wait, but it is important to realize that a better world for all of us begins with the only person that we can truly make choices for--ourselves.
